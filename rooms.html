Our hotel

Alpenhof is home to 177 hotel rooms, ranging from cosy hideouts to huge hangouts. Stay in a bed with a view or in one of our eight special rooms. Alpenhof has a lively night program on weekends. So be prepared to step in the hustle and bustle.

All our rooms have access to the following:


  • Free Wifi

  • Shower

  • Safe

  • Hairdryer
